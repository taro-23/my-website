## Description
Metallic, noisy, semi-realistic percussion sounds created by the self-oscillating sound of a frequency modulated AR filter. These sounds do not make use of AR drum synth, so they can be used on any track.
### Requirements
Analog Rytm MKI, Analog Rytm MKII

- 51 synth patches
- BD-8, Bell-3, Clap7, Cymbal-2, Hihat-2, Tambourine-1, Percussion-13, Rim shot-2, Snare-5, Zap-8
