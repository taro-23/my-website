## Description
Cycle chords are waveforms consisting of a series of notes that are integer multiples of the fundamental frequency, which approximates a chord note.
You can easily create chord stub sounds by combining filters and effects.
___
This sample pack contains 40 different chord sounds including unison and bass notes. In addition, sample chains of 40 waveforms are included for Analog Rytm, model:samples and Octatrack. Sample chains for Octatrack consist of 48 samples with 8 blanks.
#### Requirements
Sampler
#### Requirements for Chord Chains
Analog Rytm MKI, Analog Rytm MKII, Model:Samples, Digitakt2, Octatrack MKI/MKII
### Included
- 350 Single cycle chords[40 chord tones]
- Chord Chains for Elektron Analog Rytm, Model:Samples, Digitakt2 and Octatrack
- 40 Template Sounds for Analog Rytm
#### Format
- 48kHz-16bit-Wav[Available for Analog Rytm, Digitakt1/2, model:samples]
- 44.1khz-16bit-Wav [Available for Octatrack]
