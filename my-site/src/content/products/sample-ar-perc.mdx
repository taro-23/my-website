## Description

This pack is a collection of samples painstakingly created in Elektron Analog Rytm.

It includes the percussion sounds from the previously released AR Percussion Patch Pack, as well as newly created 3-voice Dual VCO chord and unison sounds. Then, a percussion sound is added that utilizes the self-oscillating sounds of the three filters to provide a semi-realistic percussion sound.

#### **AR Percussion Sample Pack features:**
- Includes sounds from the previously released AR Percussion Patch Pack and newly created Dual VCO synth sounds
- Includes percussion sounds using the self-oscillating sounds of three filters
- Synth samples include chord and unison sounds with 3 Dual VCO voices
- Percussion sounds include bell sounds and percussion (congas, tablas, etc.) using Dual VCO modulation mode
- A variety of percussion sounds created using BD synths, snare synths, rim-shot synths, etc. are provided
