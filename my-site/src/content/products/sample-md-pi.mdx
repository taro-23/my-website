## Description
### **One Shot Sample Pack+ 3 Special Oscillators**

This collection of samples was created with Elektron Machinedrum P-I machine and GND machine.

#### **P-I** = Physical Modeling Synthesis for Acoustic Drums  
P-I synth is a physical modeling synthesis for acoustic drums. Bass drum, Snare drum, Rim Shot,Crash Cymbal, Ride Cymbal,  Hihat, Maracas, metalica and Tom are included.

#### **GND**
GND is not an MD synth, but a sine wave and noise sound. Includes percussion and FX sound using MD amplified modulation and sample rate reduction.

#### **Digital Oscillator Pack**
Digital Oscillator Pack is a collection of special single cycle waveforms in three categories. The entire waveform can be looped to be used as an oscillator. The tune of waveforms is set to C.
- Chord Waves - Single Cycle Chord Waveforms
- Unison Waves - Single Cycle Unison waveforms of 3, 5, and 7 voices
- Morphing Waves - Loopable morphing waveforms
