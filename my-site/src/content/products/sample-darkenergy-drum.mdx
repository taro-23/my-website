## Description
#### **One Shot Sample Pack + Project for Elektron Analog Rytm**
This pack contains a wealth of filter-modulated sounds with oscillators that take advantage of the Doepfer Dark Energy Mk1's inherent capabilities. 
It offers a wide range of variations from drum percussion and metallic sounds to multiply recorded synth sounds.
#### **Dark Energy Drum Sample Pack features:**
- Focuses on filter modulation sounds from Doepfer Dark Energy Mk1 oscillator.
- Extensive collection of creative drum percussion sounds and unique metallic sounds.
- Multitracked synth sounds for a rich sonic experience.
